# BegTera Child Safety Standards

## Commitment to Child Safety

BegTera is an **18+ dating application**. We are fully committed to preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) on our platform and maintaining a safe environment for all users.

We strictly prohibit any content, behavior, or activity that involves or exploits minors.

---

## Age Requirements

- BegTera is intended **only for users aged 18 years and older**
- Users who are identified or reported as being under 18 are **immediately removed**
- We take reasonable measures to prevent minors from accessing the platform

---

## Prohibited Content and Behavior

The following are strictly forbidden on BegTera:

- Any sexual content involving minors
- Images, videos, messages, or profiles depicting or implying underage users
- Grooming, solicitation, or exploitation of minors
- Attempting to bypass age restrictions

Accounts found violating these rules are **permanently banned**.

---

## Reporting and Moderation

BegTera provides **in-app reporting tools** that allow users to report:

- Profiles
- Messages
- Images or media
- Any suspected child safety concerns

All reports are reviewed promptly. Content and accounts violating child safety policies are removed without notice.

---

## Enforcement and Cooperation with Authorities

- We investigate all child safety reports seriously
- We preserve relevant data where legally required
- We cooperate with **regional and international law enforcement authorities** when necessary
- We comply with all applicable child safety and CSAE prevention laws
