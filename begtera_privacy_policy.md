# Privacy Policy for BegTera

**Last Updated:** February 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to BegTera ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

By using BegTera, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information You Provide

When you create an account and use BegTera, we may collect:

- **Account Information:** Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, gender
- **Profile Information:** Photos, bio, interests, religion, location preferences
- **Identity Verification:** Documents for profile verification (if applicable)
- **Communications:** Messages exchanged with other users, support requests

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data:** App features used, interactions, time spent on the app
- **Location Data:** Approximate location (with your permission) to show nearby users
- **Log Data:** IP address, access times, app crashes, and system activity

### Information from Third Parties

- **Authentication Providers:** If you sign in using Google or other third-party services, we receive basic profile information
- **Firebase Services:** Analytics and crash reporting data

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- **Provide Services:** Create and manage your account, facilitate connections with other users
- **Improve Experience:** Personalize content, suggest compatible matches
- **Communication:** Send notifications about matches, messages, and app updates
- **Safety & Security:** Verify identities, detect fraud, enforce our terms of service
- **Analytics:** Understand app usage patterns to improve our services
- **Advertising:** Display relevant advertisements (with your consent)

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

- **Encryption:** Data encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
- **Secure Storage:** Sensitive data stored using secure storage mechanisms
- **Access Controls:** Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
- **Security Monitoring:** Regular security audits and monitoring

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Update
You can access and update your profile information directly through the App settings.

### Delete Account
You can delete your account at any time through the App settings. Upon deletion:
- Your profile and personal data will be permanently removed
- Messages with other users will be deleted
- This action is irreversible

### Location Data
You can enable or disable location services through your device settings.

### Notifications
You can manage push notification preferences in the App settings or device settings.

### Marketing Communications
You can opt out of promotional communications through the App settings.
